Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Sand Pixel Puzzle Game is a single-player offline game. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Sand Pixel Puzzle Game does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone access, camera access, or any other personal data.

Network, Analytics, and Tracking

The app is designed to work offline. It does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, remote configuration, or server-based accounts.

Game Progress

The app may save game progress, settings, level unlocks, music settings, and sound settings locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Because no personal data is collected, no personal data is shared.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information. The app does not collect personal information to share.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's local data through the operating system. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no server-side personal data to request or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any updated policy will be made available on this page.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, please use the support contact listed on the App Store product page.
